Final Project

Purpose:

Welcome to ANOTHER art gallery website. Although we are one of many websites, what will make this website unique will be the experience. The user will have the chance to find inspiration for their art; they will be able to sell their art or buy someone else's art. Need some background and some history about art? We will also have the resources for that!

Audience:

Self proclaim artists or anyone wanting to know more about art.

Data sources
